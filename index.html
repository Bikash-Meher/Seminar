From Optimization to Autonomy

It's no longer just about "tuning" a model. In 2025, we have moved to Recursive Self-Improvement (RSI).

An AI system that can autonomously generate its own training data, critique its own outputs, and rewrite its own code to become smarter without human intervention.

Solving the "Data Wall"

We are running out of high-quality human data. By 2026, high-quality human text will be exhausted.

The Solution: Synthetic Data

AI must generate its own textbooks to continue learning. Gartner predicts that by 2030, synthetic data will overshadow real data in AI models.
